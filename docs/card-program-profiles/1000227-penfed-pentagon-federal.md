## BD assessment — **C-tier prospect**

**Rationale:** PenFed runs a strong in-house Visa program at $29B in assets — above the typical CU build-vs-buy threshold. They're already committed and performing well. However, they're **Visa-only**, missing the Mastercard flank entirely. Their Pathfinder flagship is positioned against premium Visa products (Sapphire, Venture), so they'd be unlikely to add a full Mastercard line. Less compelling than banks still using agent banks, but they could be a product-add conversation ("add a Mastercard-network business card to diversify interchange").

**Opening hook:** "You've built an excellent Visa-only consumer lineup — is there any interest in adding a Mastercard business card to diversify your interchange mix?"
