## BD assessment — **B-tier prospect**

**Rationale:** Huntington self-issues a full-range Mastercard program but is Mastercard-only — a known gap in network coverage. Their card loan book ($947M) relative to assets ($224B) is 0.4%, below the 1–2% peer median, suggesting underpenetration. The Voice Rewards customization positioning shows appetite for product innovation. Brim could either (a) power a new Visa product to capture wallet share they're missing or (b) modernize their existing Mastercard platform if they're on legacy FIS/TSYS infrastructure.

**Opening hook:** "You're Mastercard-only on the consumer side — curious whether you've thought about adding a Visa option. Brim can stand one up in parallel without disrupting Voice Rewards."
