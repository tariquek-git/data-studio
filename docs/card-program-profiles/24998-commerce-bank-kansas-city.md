## BD assessment — **B-tier prospect**

**Rationale:** Commerce Bank has one of the more sophisticated card programs for a $32B regional bank — dual-network, full tier range (secured through premium), active co-brand portfolio. Their 1.8% CC-loans-to-assets ratio is strong peer-median performance. The BD opportunity isn't a "rescue from agent bank" scenario (they're too sophisticated for that); it's either (a) modernization of their existing platform if they're on legacy TSYS/FIS, or (b) cross-sell to their commercial card business. Co-brand portfolio (KC Royals, KC Zoo) suggests internal marketing bandwidth for experiments.

**Opening hook:** "You have one of the deeper dual-network card lineups of any regional bank your size — curious what your roadmap looks like for the business card portfolio."
