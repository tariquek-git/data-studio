## BD assessment — **A-tier prospect**

**Rationale:** First-Citizens is the textbook Brim target. They have $229B in assets but only $295M in credit card loans (0.13% of assets vs. peer median ~1-2%). The reason is they've **outsourced card issuance to TCM Bank** — they have no in-house platform. Post-SVB integration, they have a large underserved customer base wanting premium cards. If they wanted to modernize and take the program in-house, Brim is a natural partner. The TCM relationship is a commodity agent-bank deal; the switching cost is moderate (transition existing portfolio).

**Opening hook:** "Your cards are currently issued through TCM Bank — we'd love to show you what a modern in-house program could do for your post-SVB commercial customers."
