## BD assessment — **hard target, specific angles**

**Rationale for why they're hard:**
1. They explicitly market self-issuance as their value prop. Displacement requires overturning their stated brand position.
2. Their infrastructure is already modern (Celent Model Bank recognition).
3. Pinnacle operates both sides of the industry — they probably have informed views about Brim already.
4. They're Mastercard-exclusive at scale. They won't split networks lightly.

**Rationale for why they're still a target:**
1. **Missing Visa entirely** is a strategic gap. Every other peer bank in their size range offers both networks. Brim could stand up a Visa program in parallel to their Mastercard without cannibalizing the existing business.
2. **Potential BaaS arm via CorServ** means they're already sophisticated about multi-platform issuance. A Brim conversation about "let's power a specific new program" may land.
3. **Growth posture** — 50 Coalition Greenwich awards implies they're investing in customer experience. Premium-segment product expansions are plausible.

**Opening hook — the Visa gap:**
> "You've built an excellent Mastercard-only lineup including a World Elite product. Curious whether you've considered adding a Visa-network option for customers who prefer Visa or for specific co-brand opportunities. We can stand that up alongside your existing platform without touching it."

**Opening hook — BaaS / specialty product:**
> "I see you're running a BaaS program with CorServ for fintech partners alongside your own in-house bank cards. When you want to scale a specific new program — a premium segment, a co-brand with a Nashville institution, a digital sub-brand — happy to show you where Brim fits between what you've already built and what CorServ provides."

**Opening hook — Brim self-awareness:**
> "You're one of the few banks your size that genuinely self-issues rather than outsourcing. We're not trying to replace what you've built. We partner with banks like you on specific product expansions that don't make sense to add to the core platform."
